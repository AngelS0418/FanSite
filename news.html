News in the FC Barcelona Soccer team

9 players in the football team Barcelona, are in the Top 100 greatest players in the World. That is amazing, many young soccer players would love to be in the top 100 players. Especially in one of the world's best soccer team. Like always Lionel Messi is ranked No.1 according to British newspaper 'The Guardian'.


This Saturday FC Barcelona will face Alaves for the week 18 league clash. This will be the last game before the new year ends. Barça manager Ernesto Valverde named the following players to squad to Face Alavés:

  • ter Stergen, Neto- Goalkeeper
  • N. Semedo, Piqué, Todibo, Lenglet, Wague, Jordi Alba, S. Roberto, S. Umtiti, Firpo,-Defenders
  • I. Rakitic, F. de Jong, Aleña. -Mid-fielder
  • Suarez, Messi, C. Perez, Griezmann- Striker

Barcelona is in a need to raise close to $140 million by June to avoid going into the red. They also need to get some new signings either in the Winter transfer window or maybe during the summer. There are many players that have a higher possibility that they can go out to another team such as: Artuto Vidal, Carles Aleña, Todibo, and Ivan Rakitic. They also need to look forward to contract many other soccer players that can hel improve the overall team. Some players that FC Barcelona might have in mind signing are Lautaro Martinez, Reinier, and Rodrigo Caio.
